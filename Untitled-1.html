About the Park Dinosaurs

About Jurassic World

Jurassic World is a dinosaur theme park on Isla Nublar. It brings genetically engineered dinosaurs back to life for visitors to experience.

Dinosaurs in Jurassic World

Tyrannosaurus Rex (T-Rex)

The T-Rex is a massive carnivorous dinosaur, known for its enormous size and sharp teeth. It's one of the park's main attractions.

Velociraptor

Small but deadly, Velociraptors are extremely intelligent and fast predators. They are often seen hunting in packs.

Indominus Rex

A genetically modified hybrid dinosaur created to be bigger and stronger than any other. It is highly aggressive and dangerous.

Triceratops

A large herbivorous dinosaur with three horns and a frill. Gentle in nature, it is a favorite among visitors.

Stegosaurus

A slow-moving herbivore known for its bony plates along its back and spiked tail, used for defense.

Pteranodon

A flying reptile that soars above the park. Visitors can see it glide gracefully through the sky.

Brachiosaurus

A gigantic herbivorous dinosaur with a long neck, allowing it to reach tall trees for food. Peaceful and gentle in nature.

Spinosaurus

A massive predator that spends time on land and in water. Known for the sail-like fin on its back and powerful jaws.

Ankylosaurus

Heavily armored herbivore with a clubbed tail for defense. Slow but very difficult to attack.

Parasaurolophus

A herbivorous dinosaur known for its long, curved crest used to make loud sounds. Social and usually seen in herds.

Allosaurus

A large carnivorous dinosaur that is fast and agile, often hunting medium-sized prey in packs.

Compsognathus

Small, fast, and agile carnivore. Often depicted in groups and known for hunting insects and small animals.
